# Research Participant Consent Form
# Project: Kontablo Universal Accounting Ontology — Expert Validation Study

## 1. Description of Research
You are invited to participate in a research study focusing on the validation of the Kontablo universal accounting mapping standard. The purpose of this study is to evaluate the technical accuracy and practical feasibility of a standardized multi-jurisdiction account taxonomy.

## 2. Participation Details
- **Activity:** ~60 minute technical interview.
- **Goal:** Providing expert judgment on account mappings and structural design of the Kontablo ontology.
- **Recording:** With your permission, the audio/video of this session will be recorded for transcription purposes only.

## 3. Confidentiality and Data Protection
- **Anonymization:** Your name and specific firm name will not be used in the research report or any subsequent publications. You will be identified only by a participant code (e.g., "Expert RV-001").
- **Data Use:** Anonymized data will be used to improve the Kontablo open standard and will be published in academic and industry papers.
- **Storage:** Recordings and raw transcripts will be stored in a secure, encrypted drive and will be deleted within 24 months of study completion.

## 4. Participant Rights
- Your participation is entirely voluntary.
- You may withdraw from the study at any time without providing a reason.
- You have the right to review any direct quotes attributed to your code before publication.
