# Kontablo Expert Validation: Generic Interview Script

## Overview
- **Interviewer:** [Name]
- **Participant ID:** [RV-XXX]
- **Jurisdiction:** [Country]
- **Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]
- **Duration:** 45-60 minutes

---

## 1. Introduction (5 min)
- **Greeting:** "Hello, and thank you for participating in the Kontablo validation study. My name is [Name], and I am part of the research team developing Kontablo, a universal accounting ontology."
- **Purpose:** "The goal of this interview is to validate our proposed Level 3 account mappings against your expertise in [Jurisdiction]'s accounting standards."
- **Consent:** "You've signed the consent form, but just to confirm: Are you okay with me recording this session for transcription purposes? The recording will be deleted once transcribed and anonymized."
- **Process:** "We will go through three parts: your background, a review of specific account mappings, and a discussion on the feasibility of a universal accounting standard."

---

## 2. Professional Profile (5 min)
- "Could you briefly describe your professional background and your experience with [Jurisdiction]'s accounting standards?"
- "What is your primary industry sector?"
- "How often do you deal with multi-country consolidations or international reporting standards (IFRS)?"

---

## 4. Qualitative Discussion (15 min)
- **Feasibility:** "Do you believe a universal Level 3 taxonomy of ~30 core accounts is enough to capture 90% of routine business transactions in your country? Why or why not?"
- **Complexity:** "In your experience, what is the most difficult account category to standardize across borders?"
- **Missing Elements:** "Is there anything critical missing from this list that you see every day in your practice?"

---

## 5. Closing (5 min)
- **Feedback:** "Any additional thoughts on how we can make Kontablo more useful for practitioners in [Jurisdiction]?"
- **Next Steps:** "We will synthesize these results into our research paper. Would you like to be notified when the results are published?"
- **Acknowledgment:** "Thank you again for your time and expert insight!"
