## 1. STRUCTURE

*   **Number of hierarchical levels:** The taxonomy exhibits up to **4 levels** of explicit indentation for elements within components. Beyond this, structural elements like "Axis", "Domain", and "Member" introduce further conceptual levels for organizing disclosures, making the underlying structure deeper than four visual levels.

*   **Coding format (regex pattern):**
    The main components and abstract sections are identified by a six-digit number enclosed in square brackets, followed by their name.
    *   **Regex Pattern:** `\[(\d{6})\]`
    *   Example: `[110000]` for "General information about financial statements".

*   **Total estimated accounts:** Based on the detailed extraction below, there are **1536** unique reportable elements (accounts) and structural components identified in the taxonomy.

*   **Separator used:**
    *   Within the official IFRS Accounting Standard references, a **dot (`.`)** is used (e.g., `IAS 1.51`).
    *   For the artificial 6-digit codes `[XXXXXX]`, no internal separator is used.
    *   In the generated codes for elements without an explicit `[XXXXXX]` tag (for the CSV output), an **underscore (`_`)** is used to separate the parent component's code from the element's short name (e.g., `110000_NameOfReportingEntityOrOtherMeansOfIdentification`).
