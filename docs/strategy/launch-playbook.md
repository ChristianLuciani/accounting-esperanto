# Launch Playbook — Kontablo

> **STATUS: PLACEHOLDER.** This is a scaffold, not a completed strategy. Build it out in a dedicated session (see prepare-for-publication skill, Step 8 territory). Each section below lists the questions to answer, not the answers.

## Purpose

Coordinate the first public release of Kontablo (preprint + repo) so it is discovered, cited, and amplified — not lost among the ~2,000 papers published weekly in its category. The artifact is the smaller half of the work; findability and amplification are the larger half.

## 1. Citation strategy

Goal: situate Kontablo in citable lineages so it is discoverable through citation graphs and so reviewers recognize its intellectual context.

Questions to answer in the full session:
- Which foundational works in accounting ontology, XBRL/financial data standards, and knowledge graphs should be cited to anchor the work? (Verify each exists and says what we claim — epistemic standard.)
- Which works in the agentic-economy / LLM-agent / harness-engineering space situate the agent sections? (MCP, A2A, AP2 specs; constrained-decoding literature; harness-design literature.)
- Who are the active authors whose citation increases visibility AND is intellectually honest? (Citing for visibility alone, without genuine relevance, is dishonest and backfires.)
- What is the BibTeX/citation lineage we want Kontablo to appear adjacent to in citation databases (Semantic Scholar, OpenAlex)?

Caveat: citation-for-visibility must never override citation-for-honesty. Cite what genuinely informed the work.

## 2. Technical-influencer outreach (DM strategy)

Goal: a single mention from a credible technical voice can outweigh all organic discovery. Identify and reach them with genuine, personalized notes — never bulk.

Questions to answer:
- Who works publicly on AP2/A2A/MCP, agentic infrastructure, or accounting-tech that would find Kontablo genuinely relevant? (Build the list of 20–30 specific people.)
- What is the honest, specific hook for each? (Not a pitch — a "this connects to your work on X" note.)
- What is the sequencing? (Who gets notified day-of vs who is approached after there is traction to point to.)
- Which 3–5 podcasts/newsletters are worth a direct, specific pitch? (AI-agentic and accounting-tech spaces.)

Rule: personalized, hand-written, one-by-one. No copy-paste. No links to a wall of social posts. A genuine note about their work + the relevance + a request for honest reading.

## 3. Social media / multi-channel release

Goal: coordinated same-day release across channels, each tuned to its audience.

Questions to answer:
- Long-form narrative post (~2,500 words): the problem, the personal genesis (Venezuela hyperinflation), the architecture, what's next. Where is it hosted? (Own site / Substack.)
- Twitter/X thread (12–15 posts): thesis, key findings, dashboard screenshot. Tuned for AI-infra discovery.
- LinkedIn post (~600 words): tuned for accounting/finance professionals — emphasize the multi-jurisdictional pain, NOT the agentic angle (that audience doesn't resonate with it).
- Channel sequencing on release day and the +1 to +14 day maintenance cadence (responding to every interaction; 2–3 derivative deep-dive posts).
