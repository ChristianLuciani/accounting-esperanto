\section{What does Kontablo contribute, at a glance?}
\label{sec:signature-tables}

The three tables below compress the core of this paper into citable extracts: the
three contributions (Table~\ref{tab:contributions}), the Three-Tier Resolution
Strategy (Table~\ref{tab:three-tier}), and the coverage scorecard
(Table~\ref{tab:scorecard}). Every figure in Table~\ref{tab:scorecard} is
regenerable from the committed command named in its caption; the prose body
carries the full
argument and the \emph{Remarks} (after the Evaluation) mark the honesty
boundaries.

\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\small
\begin{tabular}{p{2.9cm} p{5.4cm} p{4.6cm}}
\toprule
\textbf{Contribution} & \textbf{What it does} & \textbf{Honesty boundary} \\
\midrule
Level~3 minimum core (30 accounts) &
Represents an estimated $\sim$94\% of routine transaction volume by posting count
($\sim$99\% with a 34-account extended core). &
A model-based estimate from a committed benchmark, not a measurement of real
ledgers. \\
\addlinespace
Three-Tier Resolution Strategy &
Deterministic exact-code and keyword resolution first, then a confidence-scored
semantic AI fallback bounded by the Deterministic Boundary Library. &
Eliminates the classical accounting hallucination class at the harness level;
residual error relocates to the semantic coverage boundary. \\
\addlinespace
Co-responsibility Architecture (CRA) &
Pairs every AI mapping proposal with a mandatory human review pathway and an
append-only audit trail. &
The human operator is always the legal principal; the agent never decides
alone. \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\caption{The three contributions of Kontablo and the boundary that keeps each
claim honest.}
\label{tab:contributions}
\end{table}

\midrule
Tier~1 &
Exact local-code $\rightarrow$ UUID lookup. &
Fully deterministic and reproducible. &
Code absent or colliding $\rightarrow$ fall through. \\
\addlinespace
Tier~2 &
Keyword / disambiguation rules keyed on deterministic fields (jurisdiction,
local code, ontology node). &
Deterministic; never keys on model free-text. &
Ambiguous input $\rightarrow$ fall through. \\
\addlinespace
Tier~3 &
Confidence-scored semantic AI fallback, constrained to UUIDs that exist in the
graph. &
Ontology-as-constraint: cannot propose a non-existent account. &
Low confidence / out-of-coverage $\rightarrow$ escalate to human (CRA). \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\caption{The Three-Tier Resolution Strategy. Determinism is exhausted before any
stochastic inference is reached; out-of-coverage instruments escalate rather than
mismap.}

\end{table}
